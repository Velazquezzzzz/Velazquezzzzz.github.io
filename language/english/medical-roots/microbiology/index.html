各种微生物

# General

-bio-: life

Bacterio-/-bacter: bacteria

  • bacteriophage 噬菌体

Vir(o)-/-vir/-virus/-phage: virus, relating to virus

  • virologist
  • antivirus

Myc(o)-/Mycet(o)-/-myco-: fungus/fungi

  • mycologist 真菌学家
  • dermatomycosis 皮肤真菌病
  • onychomycosis 甲癣

# Locations

Entero-: intestines

  • enterobacteriaceae 肠杆菌科

Pneumo-: lung

  • pneumococcus 肺炎球菌

Rhabdo-: straited muscle

Adeno-: gland

  • adenovirus 腺病毒

Gono-: reproductive

  • gonorrhoea 淋病

Meningo-: meninges

  • meningococcus 脑膜炎双球菌

Crypto-: hidden

  • cryptococcus 隐球菌

# Bacterial Shapes

Staphylo-: cluster of grapes

  • staphylococcus 葡萄球菌

Strepto-: chain

  • streptobacillus 链杆菌属

Vibrio-: curved rod

-bacillus/-bacilli: rod-shaped

  • streptobacilli 链杆菌

-coccus/-cocci: round/spherical

Diplo-: double/two/paired

Spirillum-: spiral

Clostr-: spindle

Echino-: spine-like;prickle-shaped

  • echinococcus 棘球绦虫

Actino-:ray-like processes

# Processes

-pyogenes: pus-forming

  • bacterie pyogenes 化脓性细菌

Lacto-: relating to lactic acid/lactose 乳酸 / 乳糖

Aero-: air

Sporo-: spore 芽胞，孢子

# Viral Specifiers

Picorna-: Pico RNA/small RNA

Oncorna-: Onco RNA/cancer RNA

Myxo-: mucus 粘液

Parvo-: small

Retro-: backward

  • retrovirus 逆转录病毒

aspergillus 曲霉菌

  • aspergillosis 曲霉病
